❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compress a PDF to a specific size like 150KB?

Upload your PDF file, enter your desired size (e.g. 150) in the target size box, and click Compress. The tool automatically adjusts quality and resolution to hit your exact target size within 97–100% accuracy.

Is this PDF compressor really free?

Yes, 100% free with no hidden costs, no premium tiers, and no sign-up required. Built by a non-profit tech team and free forever.

Are my PDF files uploaded to a server?

No. All compression happens directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device. No data is uploaded, stored, or collected — you can verify this in your browser's Network tab.

Can I compress multiple PDFs at once?

Yes! You can upload and compress up to 10 PDF files simultaneously. Each file will be compressed to your specified target size. Download them one by one or all at once as a ZIP.

What is the maximum file size I can upload?

Each uploaded PDF can be up to 50 MB. You can upload up to 10 files at a time for batch compression.

Does compressing a PDF reduce its quality?

Our tool uses a smart two-phase algorithm that keeps JPEG quality above a minimum threshold and adjusts resolution intelligently. This means your compressed PDFs retain maximum visual clarity for the given file size. The quality loss is minimal and often imperceptible.

Does it work on mobile phones?

Yes! The tool is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. Open it in your mobile browser, upload PDFs, and compress — all from your phone.

Which browsers are supported?

The PDF compressor works on all modern browsers — Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, Safari, Opera, and Brave.
